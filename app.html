The Milk Matters Application

Download the mobile application from the Google Playstore. Please note this is a non-permanent external link.

Depot Locator

This provides donors with a map of donation drop-off depots, and indicates which depot is closest. Additional information, such as their opening hours and which Milk Matters contacts work there, is provided for some depots (as per mothers' requests).

Milk Matters News and Events Feed

The feed enables Milk Matters to share news and events with mothers.

Educational Component

The Educational Component of the app covers a variety of topics pertaining to breastfeeding, breast milk donation, and general child care. Specific topics covered include: donating breast milk, lactation, expressing breast milk, nutrition etc. The educational resources are mainly in the form of online articles, that can be conveniently read directly from the app. The articles are sourced from authorities trusted by mothers (such as KellyMom and Milk Matters). Mothers are also able to suggest content, and specifically articles, to be included in the app.

Donation Tracking Component

This allows mothers to record and track how much breast milk they have expressed for donation. The mother's donation history is displayed both textually and visually (using graphs). This allows mothers measure their donation both in terms of litres, and how many babies they have helped feed.

About Milk Matters Screen

This provides information about Milk Matters, and ways to get in contact with them.

'Become a Donor' Questionnaire

This short questionnaire assesses your eligibility to be a potential donor. It is not a screening form, and the result of the questionnaire is for your own use only. At the end of the questionnaire, you are provided with an appropriate course of action based on your result, and the option to email Milk Matters.
