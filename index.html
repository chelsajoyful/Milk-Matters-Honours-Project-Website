Welcome to the Milk Matters computer science honours project

The aim of the project itself is to develop an app for the human milk bank, Milk Matters, and their donor mothers to:

Motivate mothers to donate their excess breastmilk to Milk Matters

Be better connected with their donor mothers and create a more interactive donation experience

Provide tools and educational resources for mothers pertaining to breastfeeding, childcare and donation
